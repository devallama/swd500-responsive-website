Visual Diagrams

An infographic is a visual representation of information in a simple, easy to understand image. They are useful for when needing to quickly portray a message across, to show a large amount of data in a simple, easy to understand manner and to notice any patterns in the data shown.

Infographics can have a number of different elements within them, such as different types of graphs and charts, images, quotes and tables among others depending on the type of information or data needed to be displayed.

Image Source: http://visual.ly/infographic-infographics-0

If you want to make your information grab attention, then infographics are a great way to do that. With an appealing design, it will grab the attention of anyone nearby, who will be curious as to what's on the infographic.

However, to ensure that those reading the infographic understand what is on it, it must be simplified and easy to read. With the chose of different ways to display information, those viewing the infographic should be able to understand it, at just a glance.

There are a number of different types of infographics available depending on the type of information you want to display.
